Copyright Policy

We respect the intellectual property rights of game developers and content creators. If you believe that your copyrighted work has been used on our site without authorization, please follow the DMCA takedown procedure outlined below.

DMCA Takedown Notice

To file a DMCA takedown notice, please provide the following information:

  1. Identification of the copyrighted work: Describe the copyrighted work that you claim has been infringed, or if multiple works, a representative list.
  2. Identification of the infringing material: Provide the URL(s) of the specific page(s) on our site that contain the allegedly infringing material.
  3. Your contact information: Include your name, address, telephone number, and email address.
  4. Good faith statement: Include a statement that you have a good faith belief that the disputed use is not authorized by the copyright owner, its agent, or the law.
  5. Accuracy statement: Include a statement that the information in your notice is accurate and, under penalty of perjury, that you are the copyright owner or authorized to act on behalf of the copyright owner.
  6. Electronic signature: Provide your physical or electronic signature.

How to Submit a DMCA Notice

Send your complete DMCA notice to:

Email: dmca@chromebookunlocked.github.io
Subject Line: DMCA Takedown Request

Response to DMCA Notices

Upon receipt of a valid DMCA notice, we will:

  • Review the notice for completeness and validity
  • Remove or disable access to the allegedly infringing content
  • Notify the user who posted the content (if applicable)
  • Respond to the complainant within 48-72 hours

Counter-Notification

If you believe that content you posted was wrongly removed due to a DMCA notice, you may file a counter-notification containing:

  • Your contact information
  • Identification of the removed content and its location
  • A statement under penalty of perjury that the content was removed by mistake
  • Your consent to jurisdiction in your location
  • Your physical or electronic signature

Game Developers

If you're a game developer and would like your game featured on our site, or if you'd like to discuss licensing, please contact us. We're always happy to work with developers to properly credit and promote their work.

Repeat Infringers

We have a policy of terminating, in appropriate circumstances, access to our site for users who are repeat infringers of intellectual property rights.
