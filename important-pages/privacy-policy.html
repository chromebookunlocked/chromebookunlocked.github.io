Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 2024

Introduction

Welcome to Chromebook Unlocked Games. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you visit our website.

Information We Collect

Automatically Collected Information

When you visit our website, we may automatically collect:

  • Browser type and version
  • Operating system
  • IP address (anonymized)
  • Pages visited and time spent on pages
  • Referring website addresses
  • Game play statistics (which games you play)

Information You Provide

We do not require you to create an account or provide personal information to play games. Any information stored is:

  • Stored locally in your browser (localStorage)
  • Recently played games history
  • Cookie preferences

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain our gaming service
  • Improve user experience and site functionality
  • Analyze site usage and trends
  • Detect and prevent technical issues
  • Remember your recently played games
  • Understand which games are most popular

Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your experience. For detailed information, please see our Cookie Policy.

Third-Party Services

Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics to analyze website traffic and usage patterns. Google Analytics uses cookies to collect information about your use of our site. This information is used to compile reports and help us improve our site.

Embedded Games

Some games on our site are embedded from third-party platforms. These platforms may have their own privacy policies and may use cookies or collect data independently.

Data Storage and Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information:

  • HTTPS encryption for secure connections
  • No collection of personally identifiable information
  • Local storage for game preferences (stored on your device)
  • Anonymized analytics data

Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access any data we collect about you
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt-out of analytics cookies
  • Clear your browser's local storage at any time
  • Disable cookies in your browser settings

Children's Privacy

Our website does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. We do not require registration or personal information to play games. If you believe we have inadvertently collected information from a child, please contact us immediately.

External Links

Our website may contain links to external sites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of these external sites. We encourage you to review their privacy policies.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

Your Consent

By using our website, you consent to our privacy policy and agree to its terms.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please visit our Contact page.
