Introduction

ChromebookUnlocked.Github.io ("we", "our", "the Site") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have when using our website and embedded games.

Information We Collect

1. Automatically Collected Data

When you visit the Site, we may automatically collect anonymized technical information, such as:

  • Browser type and version
  • Operating system
  • Anonymized IP address
  • Pages you visit and time spent
  • Referring website links
  • Game usage statistics (e.g., which games you play)

2. Information You Provide

We do not require accounts, logins, or personal details. Any data stored by the Site is stored locally on your device, including:

  • Recently played games
  • Cookie and privacy preferences
  • Local gameplay data stored by certain games

How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used to:

  • Improve website functionality and performance
  • Understand which games are most popular
  • Diagnose technical issues
  • Provide a smoother user experience
  • Maintain security of the Site

Cookies and Tracking

The Site uses cookies to remember your preferences and improve your experience. Some embedded games may also use their own cookies. For more details, see our Cookie Policy.

Third-Party Services

Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use the Site. Google may collect anonymized usage data through cookies. This data is shared with us in aggregate form only and cannot be used to identify individual users.

Embedded Third-Party Games

Some games hosted or embedded on the Site come from third-party providers. These providers may collect their own data in accordance with their own privacy policies. We encourage you to review the policies of any third-party platforms whose games you play.

Data Security

We implement security measures including:

  • HTTPS encryption
  • No storage of personally identifiable information (PII)
  • Local-only storage for user preferences
  • Anonymized analytics data

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Disable cookies in your browser
  • Clear localStorage data at any time
  • Opt out of analytics cookies
  • Request information about what anonymized data we store
  • Request deletion of analytics data (if identifiable)

Children’s Privacy

The Site does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. No account creation, personal data, or identifying information is required to use the Site. If you believe we have collected information from a child, please contact us immediately.

External Links

The Site may contain links to external websites. We are not responsible for the content or privacy practices of third-party websites. Please review their privacy policies separately.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with a revised \"Last Updated\" date. Continued use of the Site indicates acceptance of any updated terms.

Contact Us

If you have questions regarding this Privacy Policy, please visit our Contact page.
