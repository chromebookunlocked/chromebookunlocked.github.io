Cookie Policy

Last Updated: December 2024

What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your computer or mobile device when you visit our website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and provide information to the website owners.

How We Use Cookies

Chromebook Unlocked Games uses cookies for the following purposes:

Essential Cookies

  • Cookie Consent: Remember your cookie preferences
  • Recently Played Games: Store your gaming history locally for quick access
  • Site Functionality: Enable core features of our gaming platform

Analytics Cookies

  • Google Analytics: Help us understand how visitors use our site
  • Performance Monitoring: Track site performance and identify issues
  • Usage Statistics: Analyze which games are most popular

Types of Cookies We Use

Session Cookies

These temporary cookies are deleted when you close your browser. They help us remember your preferences during your browsing session.

Persistent Cookies

These cookies remain on your device for a set period or until you delete them. They help us remember your preferences and recognize you when you return to our site.

Managing Cookies

You can control and manage cookies in various ways. Please note that removing or blocking cookies may impact your user experience and some features may no longer function properly.

Browser Settings

Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings. You can set your browser to:

  • Accept all cookies
  • Notify you when a cookie is set
  • Reject all cookies
  • Delete cookies when you close your browser

Our Cookie Banner

When you first visit our site, you'll see a cookie consent banner. You can:

  • Accept all cookies by clicking "Accept" or closing the banner
  • Access more settings to customize your preferences
  • Decline non-essential cookies

Third-Party Cookies

Some cookies on our site are placed by third-party services we use:

  • Google Analytics: Helps us analyze site traffic and usage patterns
  • Game Embeds: Some games may use cookies from their hosting platforms

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Know what cookies we use and why
  • Accept or reject cookies
  • Delete cookies at any time
  • Change your cookie preferences

Updates to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for legal, operational, or regulatory reasons. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
