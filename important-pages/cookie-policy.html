Cookie Policy

Last Updated: October 2025

Introduction

This Cookie Policy explains how ChromebookUnlocked.Github.io ("we", "our", "the Site") uses cookies, local storage, and similar technologies to enhance your gaming experience, analyze site usage, and ensure the website functions correctly.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites remember user preferences, analyze performance, and provide personalized features.

Technologies We Use

Our Site uses the following technologies:

  • Cookies — small data files stored by your browser.
  • LocalStorage — stores data on your device for game features and history.
  • SessionStorage — temporary storage cleared when the browser closes.
  • Third-party cookies — used by embedded games and analytics services.

How We Use Cookies

We use cookies and similar technologies to support the following categories:

1. Essential Cookies

These are required for the Site to work properly. Without them, core features may fail.

  • Cookie Consent: Saves your cookie preferences.
  • Functionality: Ensures the site loads correctly and remembers basic settings.
  • Recently Played Games (LocalStorage): Keeps track of your game history.

2. Performance & Analytics Cookies

These help us understand how visitors use the Site and how we can improve it.

  • Google Analytics: Provides anonymized traffic and usage reports.
  • Performance Tracking: Monitors load speeds and issues.
  • Game Popularity Metrics: Helps identify which games are most played.

3. Third-Party & Embedded Game Cookies

Some games hosted or embedded on the Site may set their own cookies for gameplay functionality, saving progress, or tracking analytics according to their own policies.

Types of Cookies We Use

Session Cookies

Deleted when your browser is closed; used for temporary preferences and navigation.

Persistent Cookies

Remain on your device for a defined period; used for remembering your choices and improving your experience over time.

Managing Cookies & Preferences

Browser Controls

You can manage or block cookies in your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to:

  • Block all cookies
  • Allow only certain cookies
  • Delete existing cookies
  • Clear LocalStorage and SessionStorage

Our Consent Banner

When you first visit our Site, you will be prompted to accept or customize your cookie settings.

  • Accept all cookies
  • Reject non-essential cookies
  • Customize your preferences

You can change your cookie preferences at any time by clicking Cookie Settings.

Third-Party Cookie Providers

Third parties may set cookies through our Site, including:

  • Google Analytics for traffic measurement
  • Game publishers for gameplay and save data
  • Content delivery networks (CDNs)

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Understand what cookies we use and why
  • Accept or decline cookies
  • Withdraw consent at any time
  • Delete cookies and local storage from your browser

Changes to This Cookie Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy to reflect changes in laws or our practices. We encourage you to check this page periodically for updates.
