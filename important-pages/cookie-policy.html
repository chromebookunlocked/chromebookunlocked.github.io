What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites function correctly, improve performance, remember preferences, and provide analytics information. Some cookies are essential for the site to work, while others enhance your experience.

How We Use Cookies

ChromebookUnlocked.Github.io uses cookies for the following purposes:

1. Essential Cookies

  • Cookie Consent: Saves your cookie preference choices.
  • Recently Played Games: Stores local game history to improve convenience.
  • Core Functionality: Enables important site features needed for smooth browsing and gameplay.

2. Analytics Cookies

  • Google Analytics: Provides anonymized insights into how users interact with the site.
  • Performance Monitoring: Helps detect issues and improve loading times.
  • Game Popularity Tracking: Allows us to see which games are used most often.

Types of Cookies We Use

Session Cookies

These temporary cookies are deleted when you close your browser. They help maintain site functionality and remember temporary settings.

Persistent Cookies

These remain on your device for a set duration or until deleted. They help us remember your preferences and improve your experience when returning to the site.

Managing Your Cookies

You can control or delete cookies at any time. However, disabling some cookies may affect site performance or prevent certain features from working properly.

Browser Controls

You can adjust cookie behavior directly through your browser settings, including:

  • Allowing all cookies
  • Blocking all cookies
  • Receiving alerts when a cookie is set
  • Deleting cookies when closing your browser

Cookie Banner

When you first visit the site, you may see a cookie consent banner. You can:

  • Accept all cookies
  • Customize your cookie preferences
  • Decline non-essential cookies

Third-Party Cookies

Some cookies on our website are set by third-party services we use, including:

  • Google Analytics: Used for anonymized usage statistics and traffic insights.
  • Embedded Games: Certain games may store their own cookies depending on their platform or provider.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Know what cookies we use and why
  • Accept or reject cookies
  • Change cookie settings at any time
  • Delete cookies from your browser
